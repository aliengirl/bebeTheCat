About Me

I love to sleep. In fact, that is what I do 90% of the time. I do like to eat too, but sleeping is more my thing. Especially in a cardboard box! Every once in a while I experience a sudden burst of energy and I need to run around! I love my tower toy which has little balls I can push around within it as well as my plastic springs that bounce around. Oh-- and the reflections and lights that bounce around on the walls and ceilings catch my eye too!
