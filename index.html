What is an Exotic Shorthair?

Interesting Facts About the Exotic Shorthair:

  1. The Exotic Shorthair came about by mixing a Persian with an American Shorthair. While the Exotic Shorthair possesses a very Persian temperament, being docile and sweet, this breed is a bit more adventurous and playful due to its American Shorthair lineage.
  2. Much like the Persian, the Exotic is an affectionate breed. These cats are quiet and don't demand attention. The Exotic will stare at their hooman lovingly!
  3. Unlike your average cat, the Exotic is not as independent. The Exotic cat loves to be with their hoomans and does not like to be left alone!
